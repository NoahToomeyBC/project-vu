How to Interpret

  • This map is overlayed with the employment rate, by state, with the numbers portraying the total percentage employed. So, if a state is 96% employed then the state has 4% unemployment rate.
  • The size of the markers represents the population of as it relates to state’s number of respondents.
  • The colors of the markers on this map represent the percentage of the study's population who have some form of mental illness who did not receive treatment.
  • By clicking on the layers on the top right of the map, filters of mental health by year are displayed.

Our Analysis

We can see that the percentage of respondents who did not receive treatments increases from 2019 to 2020 which coincides with the extremely low employment rates that we see in 2020. While employment improves greatly in 2021, the percentage of the study's population stays relatively similar and even increases in some cases.
